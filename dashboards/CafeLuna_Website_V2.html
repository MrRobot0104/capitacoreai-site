Our Story
We bring the warmth of a traditional Latin American cafe to your everyday routine.

Cafe Luna was born from a simple idea: that great food and great coffee can turn an ordinary morning into something you look forward to. Nestled in Tampa's Kennedy Blvd business district, we serve the kind of meals that feel like home — bold, fresh, made from scratch, every single day.

From our signature cafe con leche to our famous Cuban sandwich to the ropa vieja that keeps regulars coming back week after week — everything is crafted with care and high-quality ingredients. Whether you're grabbing a quick breakfast or sitting down for a proper lunch, you're family here.
