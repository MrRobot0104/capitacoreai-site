How to Use Your Budget Planner

This dashboard helps you track income, expenses, debt, and savings goals in one place. Everything is editable — click any blue number to change it.

Dashboard

See your key metrics at a glance with KPI cards and interactive charts showing budget distribution, income vs. expenses, and spending by category.

Budget

Set monthly budgets by category. Click any amount to edit. Add new categories or subcategories with the + buttons, and remove what you don't need with the x buttons.

Transactions

Log actual spending using the form. Transactions update the spending charts on the dashboard. Delete any transaction with the remove button.

Yearly Overview

Project your full-year finances. Click income or expense figures to edit projections. The chart visualizes trends across all 12 months.

Debt Tracker

Track all debts with balances, interest rates, and payments. Add new debts or remove paid-off ones. Progress bars show payoff progress.

Savings Goals

Set financial goals with target amounts and timelines. Track monthly contributions and see your progress toward each goal.

Tips

  • Blue numbers are clickable and editable
  • Changes update all charts and calculations instantly
  • Use Export to download your data as JSON
  • Customize categories to match your actual spending
