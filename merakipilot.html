Natural language operations

Tell MerakiPilot what you need in plain English. "Reboot the warehouse switch." "Show me offline devices." "Run a security audit."

24/7 autonomous monitoring

Scans your networks every 10 minutes. Detects offline devices, security gaps, firmware issues, and unusual client behavior automatically.

Smart auto-remediation

Configurable policies per client. Auto-reboot stuck devices, auto-enable IDS, enforce security baselines — or alert only. Your call.

Multi-org MSP support

Connect multiple Meraki organizations. Switch between clients instantly. Each org gets its own policies, alerts, and audit trail.

Webex bot interface

Chat with MerakiPilot through Cisco Webex. Get health reports, troubleshoot clients, toggle SSIDs, and manage devices — all from a message.

Confirmation-gated actions

Every write action (reboot, config change, SSID toggle) requires explicit confirmation. No surprises, full audit trail.

How It Works

Three steps to AI-managed networks.

1

Connect your Meraki API key

Paste your API key into the Webex bot or the agent CLI. MerakiPilot discovers your orgs, networks, and devices automatically.

2

Set your policies

Choose what MerakiPilot should monitor, what it can auto-fix, and where to send alerts. Configurable per client org.

3

Let it run

MerakiPilot monitors 24/7, takes action per your policies, and reports back. Jump in via Webex anytime for hands-on control.
